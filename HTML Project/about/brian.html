Brian Torok - Lead Guitarist

Brian is one of the 3 original founding members of Abide by Lies. He's been playing guitar since he was 13, and has been in 2 other (unsuccessful) bands before AbL. He brings the heavier metal sound into this strange mix of emo and thrash.
Fun fact - before joining Abide by Lies, Brian and Shannon were competing for the vocalist spot of Jeff's previous band.
